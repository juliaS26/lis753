Pasta Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup chopped onions
  • 1 (28-ounce) can whole peeled plum tomatoes
  • 12 ounces uncooked rigatoni pasta
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper
  • 1/2 ounce Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Chopped fresh flat-leaf parsley

Directions

  1. Melt butter in a large Dutch oven over medium. Add onion; cook, stirring occasionally, until tender and golden brown, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add tomatoes and their juices; bring to a boil over medium.
  3. Reduce heat to medium-low; simmer, stirring occasionally, until flavors meld and sauce thickens, about 30 minutes.
  4. Meanwhile, cook pasta in salted water according to package directions for al dente.
  5. Drain pasta, save 1/2 cup of the water.
  6. Stir cream, salt, black pepper, and crushed red pepper into tomato sauce.
  7. Add cooked pasta, and toss to coat. Add reserved pasta cooking liquid, 1/4 cup at a time, until desired consistency is reached.
  8. Divide pasta evenly among 4 bowls; sprinkle with Parmesan, and garnish with parsley.
