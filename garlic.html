Garlic Bread Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 loaf of italian bread
  • 1/2 of butter
  • 5 large cloves of minced garlic
  • 1/4 cup of parmesean

Directions

  1. preheat oven to 350 degree F
  2. cut the bread loaf in half
  3. mix the minced garlic and butter together and spread it evenly on bread
  4. place in oven for 10-15min
  5. optional: sprinkle parmesean on top
